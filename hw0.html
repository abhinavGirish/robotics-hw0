Sensing

The robot most likely uses GPS coordinates to determine its current location.

Planning

The robot probably utilizes computer vision to determine areas that have relatively few obstacles in its line of sight. It then determines what direction to steer.

Acting

The robot most likely computes a set of waypoints (2D coordinates) for a desired location, and utilizes inverse kinematics to figure out the desired angle configurations of the joints to move to the location.
